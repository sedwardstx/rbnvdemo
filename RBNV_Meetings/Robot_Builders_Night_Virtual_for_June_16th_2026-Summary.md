## Introduction
During this session of the Robot Builders Night Virtual for June 16th, 2026, participants discussed advancements in GPS technology, specifically RTK (Real Time Kinematic) systems, and shared educational resources related to robotics learning.

## Main Discussion Points

### RTK GPS Systems
- **Paul Bouchier** shared a link to his project on developing a driver for the lc29h_da RTK GPS system.
- Key aspects discussed included the unit price of RTK components, with each the rover and the base station priced at $73.
- The necessity of a base station depends on individual project requirements.

### Educational Resources
